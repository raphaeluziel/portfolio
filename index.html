Hard Worker in Software

    For the past few years I have been learning and exploring many areas of computer science. My area of strength is back end web development using Flask and Django, but more recently computer science, C++, algorithms, and Java have been my primary focus. And dabbling in computer systems administration has led to this website, whose server I manage.

    I have been teaching physics in high school for 30 years, and have cherished interacting with my students and trying to instill in them a passion for the sciences. However, I also love computer science and look forward to learning more, perhaps putting what I learn to good use after I retire from teaching.

    I began my journey into coding with freecodecamp, earning their Full Stack Certification. Then I completed two online courses offered by Harvard University through edX, CS50x: Introduction to Computer Science, and CS50w: Web Programming with Python and Javascript. More recently, I have been learning C++ on my own using the book C++ Primer by Lippman, Lajoie and Moo, and studying computer algortithms with Robert Sedgewick through Princeton's Algorithms course on Coursera.
